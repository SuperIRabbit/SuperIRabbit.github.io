I joined Facebook/Meta in October 2016 as a Research Scientist focusing on computer vision and machine learning. Before joining Facebook, I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Southern California in 2016. At USC, I worked in the Computer Graphics and Immersive Technologies (CGIT) Lab, advised by Suya You. Before attending USC, I received my B.S. degree from School of Software, Tsinghua University in 2010.

I'm also broadly interested in related fields including computer graphics, artificial intelligence, robotics and algorithms.
